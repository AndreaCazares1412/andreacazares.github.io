"Yet-to-be": A Space for Inspiration & Pop Culture Reflections | 2022

TikTok project designed to inspire and entertain, producing reels that dive into trending topics and cultural reflections. Tailored for Latin American Millennials and Gen Z women, the content stands out for its storytelling, relatable voice, and vibrant illustrations. Themes often explore dating terms, beauty ideals, imposter syndrome, and FOMO, all presented with a fresh, thought-provoking twist. Driven by a passion for content creation and engaging storytelling, "Yet-to-be" is a dynamic space where culture, reflection, and entertainment meet.

Focus Travel: A Full-Stack Development Experience | Apr 2021 – Oct 2021

Through this experience, I gained hands-on skills in various tools and technologies (e.g., React.js, Node.js, Git, MySQL).
